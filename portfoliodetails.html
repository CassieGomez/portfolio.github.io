Her Solo Compass

Her Solo Compass was designed to be a resource of inspiration and information for solo female travelers. The HTML content aims to empower women to confidently solo travel by offering tips, destination guides, safety advice, and personal stories from other solo female travelers. Additionally, Her Solo Compass will offer various services such as travel planning assistance, and customized itineraries.

CSS used in the website design is aimed to be visually immersive, featuring a carousel of stunning photography with diverse landscapes, destinations, and confident, solo female travelers. Warm and neutral tones were used to evoke a sense of comfort, sophistication, and adventure. Images that contained a solo women were important to the layout. Some images chosen also included the color yellow to represent happiness and optimism which is an emotion this website aims to inspire in its users.
